subroutine SPEC3D (PROCNUM, IN1, IN2, IN3, NX, NY, NZ, OUT)
 Computes the three dimensional energy spectrum as the fourier transformation
of the auto-correlation function. In the first step all three velocity
components are fourier transformed. The second step consists of multiplying
each transform with its conjugate complex in order to get the elements of the
main diagonal of the velocity spectrum tensor

After the components of have been determined an integration over
spherical shells has to be computed in order to get the three dimensional
velocity spectrum.

Computes the three dimensional energy spectrum as the fourier transformation
of the auto-correlation function. In the first step all three velocity
components are fourier transformed. The second step consists of multiplying
each transform with its conjugate complex in order to get the elements of the
main diagonal of the velocity spectrum tensor

After the components of have been determined an integration over
spherical shells has to be computed in order to get the three dimensional
velocity spectrum.
